Overview

This year, NMU-CHINA used biosynthetic methods to create LuminAid, an innovative light-emitting bacterium. We plan to incorporate it into our spacesuits to provide a precise positioning solution for astronauts in deep space exploration. At the same time, it also shows great potential for applications on the planet. We can terrasify it and equip it with a life jacket to accurately locate the location of the person who has fallen into the water, which greatly improves the efficiency and accuracy of sea rescue.

In order to translate this innovative idea into tangible results for the benefit of humanity and successfully apply it to the field of space exploration and maritime safety, our team has conducted in-depth research on the demand gap of potential customers and conducted comprehensive and detailed market insights over the past few months, mainly in the field of life jackets. Based on this, we analyzed and planned the functional characteristics of LuminAid's products, the expertise of our team, and the path to future commercialization. In order to achieve the realization from the lab to the market, our team has worked tirelessly on the business strategy to ensure that LuminAid is not only a theoretical innovation, but also a social force that solves practical problems and creates value.

Client

The background, pain points, opportunities, target customers, stakeholders, and product users are elaborated here.

1.Background:

The total area of the Earth's oceans is about 360 million square kilometers, or about 71% of the Earth's surface area. With the development of the ocean and the increasing frequency of maritime activities, the number of people in distress at sea has also increased significantly. In the first two months of 2024, maritime search and rescue centers at all levels across the country organized and coordinated a total of 195 search and rescue operations, rescuing a total of 1,299 people in distress[1]. In addition, the survival rate of personnel in distress at sea is relatively low: personnel distress often occurs in the vast sea area, which makes the person who falls into the water easily affected by currents, waves and tides, and quickly moves away from the place of the incident, which greatly increases the difficulty of rescue positioning. At the same time, drifting at sea for long periods of time can lead to dehydration, starvation, and hypothermia, so it is important to find the victim quickly and accurately.

2.Pain point

In recent years, there has been an alarm and positioning device for people overboard at sea based on inertial guidance, wireless communication, satellite positioning and navigation and other technologies and gradually equipped with large ships[2], although compared with the early light strip, smoke, position indicator light, whistle and other devices have greatly improved the rescue efficiency, but there are still a series of shortcomings such as high cost, poor stability, rough range, high power consumption, short standby time, poor portability (see product module for details). How to design a stable, low-energy, strong-signal, and accurate signal mode has become a difficult problem for people to tackle.

3.Opportunity

The problems described in the pain points also cause us NMU-igemers to think. By chance, we saw the beautiful scenery of Dalian fluorescent sea on the Internet, and we had a flash of inspiration, whether we can equip this fluorescent to the life jacket, through the means of synthetic biology, so that it can have a strong light without external energy! That's why we designed our product – a life jacket with LuminAid.

4.Target Customers

IGEM NMU designs life jackets with LuminAid for commercial shipping companies, fishing vessels, government and sea rescue agencies, recreational yacht and cruise ship passengers, and water sports enthusiasts, among others. These companies offer a wide range of ocean freight services across the globe, covering a wide range of sectors from container transportation to bulk cargo transportation. They are industry leaders who are willing to take responsibility for the safety of people at sea, thus investing in our products. Finally, they should agree to our security and responsibility format and adhere to it throughout the cooperation process. For example, COSCO Shipping, Hapag-Lloyd, United States Presidential Steamship (APL), Japan Yusen (NYK) and ships are the main customers we are considering in the future. Of course, in addition to these large companies, small companies are also potential users.

Products

Product, competition, market analysis, cost analysis, and intellectual property are elaborated here.

1.Product description

(1)Product name

Fluorescent Positioning Life Jacket.

(2)Product Introduction

This product is a combination of innovative technology and safety concept of fluorescent positioning life jacket, designed for marine operators and water sports enthusiasts. In the event of a shipwreck or emergency, the fluorescent positioning life jacket can quickly respond to environmental changes, and the built-in freeze-dried bacterial powder package emits blue fluorescence when activated by water, significantly improving the rescue positioning efficiency and winning valuable survival time for those in distress.

(3)Application Scenarios

Maritime navigation and operation: For fishermen, seafarers and offshore oil exploration workers who have been working at sea for a long time, intelligent fluorescent positioning life jackets can automatically send out fluorescent signals in emergency situations to help search and rescue teams quickly locate.

Water sports and leisure: Suitable for surfing, windsurfing, kayaking and other water sports enthusiasts, in the event of accidental falling into the water or lost, the automatic fluorescence function can greatly improve the chance of being found.

Shipwreck rescue: In shipwreck accidents such as ship sinking and personnel falling into the water, the fluorescent positioning function of the life jacket has become a key life guide, providing important support for survival and rescue.

(4)Project Structure

①Main material: made of high-strength, waterproof polyester fiber material.

②Buoyancy system: built-in multiple high-density closed-cell foam materials to ensure that wearers can keep their heads above water even when tired or unconscious, in line with international safety standards.

③Reflective strip, reflective whistle and fluorescent sign: the front and back of the life jacket and the side are equipped with high-brightness reflective strip, which can be found remotely at night or in low-light environments to improve safety. At the same time, the built-in safety whistle can emit a loud whistle in emergency time to attract the attention of the surrounding personnel and increase the chance of rescue.

④Quick wear system: The one-pull design allows the wearer to quickly put on the life jacket in case of emergency. Plastic fasteners and adjustable nylon webbing ensure that the pocket does not misopen during operation.

⑤Freeze-dried bacterial powder pack: The front storage bag of the life jacket is equipped with freeze-dried bacterial powder wrapped by folded nanofibers. (The nanofibers are based on polypropylene and made by electrostatic spinning technology) When the nanofiber is unrolled, the freeze-dried bacteria powder in it can be activated by water to produce fluorescence. Among them, the nanofibers are mainly prepared by electrostatic spinning technology, which has good biocompatibility and the characteristics of simulating natural extracellular matrix, and is suitable for algal bacteria system to maintain activity on the sea surface for a long time.

⑥PFD quick release device: there is a traction rope connecting pull ring on the back, which is used to connect the quick release traction rope and escape quickly in distress.

(5)How to Use

Wear: Take the life jacket out of the storage bag and put it on quickly through the quick wear system.

Unfold: After wearing, pull down the handle to unfold the filter membrane containing freeze-dried bacterial powder content in the storage bag. The freeze-dried bacterial powder is activated when it meets water and expresses the balloon protein at the same time, making the filter membrane most likely to unfold on the water surface and form a blue fluorescent circle.

Wait for rescue: Use the buoyancy of the life jacket to stay afloat, while sending a distress signal and waiting for rescue.

(6)Product Features

①Advantages:

Safety, Reliability, Practicality: high-strength waterproof material and buoyancy system to ensure the safety of the wearer.

Quick effect: no need for the manual operation.It brightens when it meets water, thus improving rescue efficiency.

Wide range of applications: Marine navigation and operation, water sports and leisure, maritime rescue.

Double visibility: A combination of reflective strips, fluorescent markers and automatic fluorescence for increased visibility 24/7.

②Disadvantages:

Higher cost.

At present, the alarm and positioning devices for people overboard at sea are mainly divided into the following four categories:①alarm and positioning devices based on radio measurements;②Alarm positioning device based on wireless communication;③Alarm positioning device based on global positioning and wireless communication;④Alarm positioning device based on Beidou navigation system[2].

It causes the shortcomings of inconvenient equipment use, long training time, high judgment error rate, and long positioning time.

In the middle and high latitudes, due to the small number of Beidou visible satellites and poor satellite distribution, the positioning accuracy is poor or cannot be located

There are many challenges in the technical development and application popularization of marine alarm positioning devices, and our products can better complement these alarm positioning devices:

①The engineering bacteria are autotrophic, do not need to be artificially provided with energy from the outside, and last longer. And only a small packet of freeze-dried powder and life jackets can be carried, which is miniaturized and convenient. But at the same time, the range of light is large enough and the brightness is bright enough to allow rescuers to find the victims in time.

②The existing radiocommunication technology and the Global Navigation Satellite System (GNSS) are susceptible to interference and tampering, such as interference from complex maritime environments or interference from satellite distribution, while our engineering bacteria will not be affected by external factors. It surrounds the person who fell into the water and accurately locates the specific person who fell into the water, and is not limited by the communication location.

③Even if the various alarm positioning devices are accurate, it is difficult to accurately locate the individual, and can only locate a rough position, while our engineering bacteria can accurately locate the encountered personnel.

Overall, our engineered bacteria can be applied together with other marine rescue equipment to complement each other. Other marine alarm positioning technologies can achieve a rough range of positioning, while our engineering bacteria can accurately locate the specific person who has fallen into the water, which is also the pain point of our product directly hitting the user, and it is the unique selling point of the product.

3.Market analysis

In recent years, although the overall trend of marine accidents around the world has slowed down, the number remains high[4]. About 200,000 people die each year as a result of shipwrecks, of which 50,000 drown directly after a shipwreck, and another 50,000 die on life-saving equipment in non-extreme conditions[5]. This data reveals the importance of timely rescue of those killed, as well as the potential market for our products. According to statistics, the market size of the life jacket industry is showing a growing trend, and it is estimated that by 2025, the global market size will reach 8 billion US dollars[6]. In recent years, thanks to the promotion of downstream demand, China's life jacket market has developed rapidly, and the industry is currently in a period of steady development. In the future, it is expected that with the development of boats, airplanes and other means of transportation, as well as rafting, fishing, and water sports, there is still room for growth in the market demand for life jackets in China[7]. In addition, there are no competitors or sales of the same products in the Chinese market, our future market size is relatively large, and the market revenue is relatively good.
